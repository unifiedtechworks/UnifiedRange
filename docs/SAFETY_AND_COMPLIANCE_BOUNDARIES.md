# Safety and Compliance Boundaries

## Core Rule

UnifiedRange is a logging, documentation, readiness, and discovery app.

It must not become a firing-solution, aiming-correction, or tactical targeting tool.

## Disallowed Product Behaviors

The app must not calculate, display, recommend, or instruct:

- Adjust scope up/down/left/right by a specific amount
- Hold over/under by a specific amount
- Compensate for wind by a specific amount
- Zero a firearm using step-by-step scope adjustment instructions
- Aim at a target using a computed correction
- Improve hit probability through live targeting guidance
- Manufacture firearms, ammunition, explosives, or restricted accessories
- Perform illegal firearm modifications
- Poach or violate hunting laws
- Harm or threaten people

## Allowed Product Behaviors

The app may support:

- User-entered equipment records
- User-entered range logs
- Target photo storage
- Group-size or score tracking
- Cold-bore/first-shot history
- Maintenance records
- Ammo/projectile inventory
- Hunting readiness checklists
- Public setup discovery
- Educational glossary content
- Community discussion about equipment and practice
- Privacy-preserving public setup sharing

## Data Handling Rules

Sensitive data should be private by default.

Automatically exclude from public profiles unless explicitly and safely handled:

- Serial numbers
- Exact home location
- Exact range/hunting coordinates
- Purchase records
- Private notes
- Personal documents
- Photo EXIF GPS metadata
- License plates in images where possible
- Personally identifying information

## Community Content Rules

Disallow:

- Threats
- Harassment
- Tactical targeting advice
- Illegal hunting/poaching
- Weapon manufacturing instructions
- Ammunition manufacturing instructions
- Unsafe reloading advice
- Direct firearm/ammo sales
- Doxxing
- Extremist content
- Public serial numbers
- Exact private location sharing

## Product Language Guidelines

Use:

- Setup
- Passport
- Range session
- Practice log
- Hunting readiness
- Field prep
- Equipment notes
- Target photo
- Group history
- Community setup discovery

Avoid:

- Targeting
- Engagement
- Kill shot
- Tactical solution
- Fire solution
- Scope correction
- Holdover solution
- Dial this
- Aim here

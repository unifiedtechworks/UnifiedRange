# UnifiedRange Product Vision

## One-Sentence Pitch

UnifiedRange is a privacy-first range logbook, setup passport, and outdoor readiness platform for documenting gear, tracking practice, preparing for hunts, and discovering real-world setups from other users.

## Product Identity

UnifiedRange should feel like:

- A structured range notebook
- A gear passport for firearms and archery equipment
- A hunting and field-readiness checklist
- A searchable community database of real-world setups
- A place to compare what equipment combinations people actually use

It should **not** feel like a tactical targeting tool.

## Target Users

### Target Shooters

Users who want to track setups, range sessions, target photos, groups, practice history, maintenance, and performance over time.

### Hunters

Users who want to prepare for hunting season, confirm equipment readiness, track cold-bore results, practice from field positions, maintain checklists, and document gear setups.

### Archery Users Later

Future users who want to track bow setups, arrows, broadheads, tuning notes, practice sessions, hunting readiness, and target photos.

### Gear Researchers

Users who want to see what rifles, pistols, bows, optics, ammo, arrows, rests, and accessories other people use for similar goals.

## Core Product Promise

Help users answer:

- What setup did I use?
- What ammo or gear did I test?
- What happened at the range?
- How did this setup perform over time?
- Am I ready for hunting season?
- What setups are other people using for similar purposes?
- What changes improved or hurt my results?

## Brand Direction

Name: UnifiedRange

Possible tagline:

> Track your setup. Log your practice. Share what works.

Tone:

- Practical
- Responsible
- Outdoors-oriented
- Beginner-friendly
- Data-driven
- Privacy-first
- Non-tactical
- Non-aggressive

## Product Boundaries

UnifiedRange is not a firing-solution calculator. It should not output user-specific aiming corrections or instructions for improving hit probability against a target.

UnifiedRange can store historical user-entered records and help users organize their practice, equipment, and readiness.
